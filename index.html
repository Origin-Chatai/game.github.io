URG ✘ PUMP

URG is an FPS that combines modern and retro shooter elements. You can carry 6 types of weapons and gadgets, switching between them using the weapon key or weapon wheel with slow motion. The game includes modern mechanics like sprints, wall runs, leans, and sliding, but maintains a classic episodic format without a story. Each episode includes 5 levels and 1 secret level.

Key Features

  • Combo System: Earn ranks by defeating enemies in a row, unlocking classic controls and buffs.
  • Recovery via Gibs: Restore health and combo gauges by collecting gibs from defeated enemies.
  • Dual-Wielding: Freely combine two-handed weapons for tactical advantage.
  • Custom Engine: Rendered using GPU-based raycasting with 64x64 pixel textures.
